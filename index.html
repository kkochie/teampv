Team Pleasant Valley

Team Pleasant Valley represents the best in public service our town has to offer. We are a bipartisan group, united to serve the community responsibly, transparently and respectfully.

Service

Regardless of party affiliation, we are committed to listening to all residents, first to understand concerns and then to make improvements that yield a tangible and positive result.

Results

Team Pleasant Valley has one unifying mission, to deliver on long-standing improvements needed by our town. Whether it's a new approach to town water and the town hall project, or enhancing recreation and local business, this team is united to deliver results.

Community

Time and time again, when it comes to local politics, we hear residents say, “It's not about the party, it's about the individuals and how they plan to serve.” Team Pleasant Valley is a direct reflection of what people want in their local government. No matter your background, you have an advocate on Team Pleasant Valley.
